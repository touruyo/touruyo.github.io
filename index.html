About Uyo

The vibrant capital of Akwa Ibom State, known as the "Land of Promise"

Uyo is the state capital of Akwa Ibom, located in South South Nigeria. The city became the capital of the state on September 23, 1987 following the creation of Akwa Ibom State from erstwhile Cross River State.

As the most popular city in south-south Nigeria, Uyo is a local government area and the capital of Akwa Ibom - 'Land Of Promise', a major oil producing state. An activity-packed destination featuring culture, outdoor activities, fine museums, attractions and miles of beaches.

Uyo presents a picture of enchanting coastal landscapes, mangrove forests, beautiful sand beach resorts, and other interesting places one can visit. Whether you're an adventure seeker, movie lover, golfer, foodie, shopaholic or collector of random facts, you will find there are so many things to do while visiting this remarkable city.

Must-Visit Attractions

Discover the most iconic and beautiful places in Uyo

IBOM UNITY PARK

Also known as Cenotaph to the indigenes of Uyo, Unity Park serves as a public park and cenotaph for fallen heroes. The Unity Park serves as a multipurpose centre playing host to different activities on Saturdays from weddings and football matches to yoga. The Armed Forces Remembrance Day holds here annually, making it a significant cultural and historical landmark.

GODSWILL AKPABIO INTERNATIONAL STADIUM

The Godswill Obot Akpabio stadium is a magnificent sight to behold. The design and facilities found in the Godswill Akpabio stadium put it on the same pedestal with many international stadiums. The stadium is popularly referred to as "The Champions' nest" as it serves home to the super eagles of Nigeria. When not in use for football-related activities, the stadium hosts various cultural, religious and social events. The 30,000 seater multi-purpose sporting complex was modelled in the image of the Allianz Arena.

IBOM LE-MERIDIEN HOTEL

The Le Meridien Ibom is one of the most beautiful hotels in Nigeria and has been attracting tourists from all around since its commissioning. Le Meridien is a great place for a company retreat with its lush vegetation and world-class 18 hole golf course. Renowned for providing the best hospitality money can buy anywhere in Nigeria, it offers a luxurious escape with stunning views and exceptional service.
