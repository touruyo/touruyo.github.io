Background of the study

Tourism involves movement to a place on a temporary basis and incurring some expenses at the destination without making a profit. Geographic Information System(G.I.S) have been seen to have applications in many areas of managing information that has both spatial and non-spatial context and other studies have shown that it is possible to apply GIS to tourism.

About Uyo

Uyo is the state capital of Akwa Ibom, in South South Nigeria. The city became the capital of the state on September 23, 1987 following the creation of Akwa Ibom State from erstwhile Cross River State. The most popular city in south-south Nigeria, Uyo is a local government area and the capital of Akwa Ibom- ‘Land Of Promise’, a major oil producing state. An activity-packed destination featuring culture, outdoor activities, fine museums, attractions and miles of beaches, Uyo presents a picture of enchanting coastal, mangrove forest, beautiful sand beach resorts, and other interesting places one can visit. Whether an adventure seeker , movie lovers, golfer, foodie, shopaholic or collector of random facts, you will find there are so many things to do while visiting.


Tools Used

Below is the description of each tools that was used for this project

QGIS

QGIS is a free and open-source GIS application that supports viewing, editing, and analysis of geospatial data.

QGIS2Web Plugin

qgis2web generates a web map from your current QGIS project, as OpenLayers or Leaflet.

Leafjetjs

Leaflet is the leading open-source JavaScript library for mobile-friendly interactive maps.

Arc GIS

Create a map that can be viewed in a browser, desktop or mobile device. Share it on a blog embed it in a website.

IBOM UNITY PARK

Also known as Cenotaph to the indigenes of Uyo, Unity Park serves as a public park and cenotaph for fallen horses. The Unity Park serves as a multipurpose centre playing host to different activities on Saturdays from weddings and football matches to yoga. The Armed Forces Remembrance Day holds here annually.

GODSWILL AKPABIO INTERNATIONAL STADIUM

The Godswill Obot Akpabio stadium is a magnificent sight to behold. The design and facilities found in the Godswill Akpabio stadium put it on the same pedestal with many international stadiums. The stadium is popularly referred to as “The Champions’ nest” as it serves home to the super eagles of Nigeria. When not in use for football-related activities, the stadium hosts various cultural, religious and social events. The 30,000 seater multi-purpose sporting complex was modelled in the image of the Allianz Arena.

IBOM LE-MERIDIEN HOTEL

The Le Meridien Ibom is one of the most beautiful hotels in Nigeria and has been attracting tourists from all around since its commissioning. Le Meridien is a great place for a company retreat with its lush vegetation and world-class 18 hole golf course. Renowned for providing the best hospitality money can buy anywhere in Nigeria.
